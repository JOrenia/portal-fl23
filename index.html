About Me

My name is Jereck Orenia, and I'm currently enrolled at Seattle Central College through the Year Up program. With a passion for everything tech-related and an insatiable curiosity that fuels my quest for knowledge, I'm on the path to becoming a software developer in test. While I'm dedicated to my studies, I'm also currently serving as an assistant general manager at the Lakewood Racquet and Sports Club. In this role, I assist with various administrative tasks, including scheduling, consulting, website management, financials, and member relations.

In my free time, you'll often find me absorbed in my many hobbies and activities. I'm an avid tennis player with over a decade of experience, and I aspire to share the joy of tennis with others. My love for tinkering with all things tech is a driving force behind my pursuit of a career as a software developer. Additionally, I'm an aspiring photographer, that loves to capture moments that will be cherished forever. You can follow my photographic journey on my personal Instagram page, where I share glimpses of my everyday life.

As I continue to progress on my journey towards becoming a software developer and tester, I hope to continue to form new connections and create new memories along the way.
